Grow & Sow


Frustrated with the lack of easy-to-access data on growing vegetables at home in the UK, I created a web app to fulfil my needs.

Users can select either a crop or month of their choice. The crop selection will display which months are suitable for indoor growth, outdoor growth and harvesting. Selecting a month will show you what vegetables you could plant or harvest at that time of year.
