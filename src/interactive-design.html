Emoti


A light and sound installation representing the emotional state of Twitter, created for #ArtHackathon 2015.

Twitter widget Data alters the colours and music according to each emotional response, in real time, through a Raspberry Pi. The 3D version has been dismantled (for now), but the web experience lives on.

Sow & Grow


Frustrated with the existing web content on growing vegetables at home in the UK, I created this web app to help fulfil my needs.

The app holds data for indoor, outdoor and harvesting seasons, allowing you to discover what fruits or vegetables you should be planting at any given time of year.
