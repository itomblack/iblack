Agile Metrics App

The problem

How do you track Agile adoption levels within a global organisation?

As a clients at pebble {code} continued it's Agile transformation, its managers wanted a way to track team progress at following the Agile methodology.

The method

We began with an analysis of their existing methods. Teams rated themselves against set criteria, which was converted in to a spider diagram.

Next, we hosted a workshop with the key stakeholders to understand the requirements of this system, from the user's perspective. We identified three main personas.

  • The Team Member
  • The Agile Coach
  • The Agile Manager

We mapped out the ideal process, which engaged teams in retrospectives with their agile coach, guided by an app. This would allow consistent data to be tracked across all teams. Along side the data, contextual information would be collected, to highlight areas of weakness, and actions to improve them. The app would visualise team progress over the year, while managers would be able to view aggregated reports to see how the company was doing as a whole.

After mapping the ideal, we honed it down to a Minimal Viable Product. Using node & ReactJS, the developers & I built a mobile first, interactive assessment tool. Quaterly progress was visualised through D3 charts. An admin section allowed the client to add, edit and remove teams from the system.

The result

App currently being trialled with teams.
