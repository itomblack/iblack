The Problem

How do you make an incredibly hard game about hex colours?

The Process

Another day, another pebble {code} hack day. This time, the theme was gaming. I chose to build a mobile friendly colour matching game, using JQuery Mobile.

The Product

To win, you have to try and convert Hex to HSL in your head, and move the sliders to the right combination. The closer the match, the higher the score.
