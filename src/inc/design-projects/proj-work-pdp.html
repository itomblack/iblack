The problem

How do you enable staff to track their professional development?

The Process

A new framework for personal development & remuneration was being rolled out at pebble {code}. It mapped ideal traits - Skills, Behaviours & Responsibilities - against which staff could be rated from zero to three.

User research was conducted with those who had been through the initial review process, to understand the areas of friction in the existing process. The main problems highlighted were:

  • Difficulty navigating the spreadsheet of traits.
  • Lack of clarity in the rating system.
  • Difficulty visualsing their current state.

Using these insights, an MVP app was mapped out to address these concerns. A personal desktop app was built using ReactJS. Accounts were managed through Google authentication, enabling users to rate, record and visualise where they are on their career path.

Usabilty testing was performed before the launch, and a more detailed user testing plan was put together for after the review process. The results of this are currently being collected...
