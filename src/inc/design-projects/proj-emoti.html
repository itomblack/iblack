The Problem

How would you visualise the Emotional state of the world?

The Process

At #ArtHackathon2015, our team of artists and designers created a possible solution, in the form of a light and sound installation, powered by tweets.

Using the Twitter post fetcher, I altered the colours and music of a webpage according to each emotional response of tweets, in real time. This was then visualised on the browser of a Raspberry Pi. Using lazer-cut acryclic mounted on to an LED screen, a glowing, morphing sculpture was created.

The Product

The 3D version has been dismantled (for now), but feel free to check out the code behind it.
