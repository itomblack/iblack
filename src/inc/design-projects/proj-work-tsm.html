The problem

How do you bring a product back to life through design thinking?

The Process

Introduction

The Package Holidays function of TravelSupermarket was in need of a shake up. I am working on the product team responsible for delivering their new strategy & an improved design process. We have 3 months to deliver a measurable change.

Stakeholder Interviews

You can't expect to change an organisation without first trying to understand the key players within it. We spent the first week interviewing STLs, Data Scientists, BA's and Designers. We quicly saw where our team could add the most benefit.

Understanding the User

Before I augmented the TSM design team, I needed to get a better understanding of their customers, based on existing data. I had a colleciton of existing reports to analyse, and access to the Google Analytics account.

After using up the office supply of post-its, I pieced together a portrait of the people who visit the TSM site.

Product Strategy

Based on our research and the new strategic direction for the product, we prioritised search results as the first section to be given an overhaul. This needed to be broken down into manageable chunks that could be developed in an Agile way. First up was the search result cards. I started with some competitor analysis, comparing the existing product with a numbe of rival businesses like Trivago and Kayak.

Designing for Price Comparison

The existing cards made heavy use imagery, which was often poor quality. The mobile experience felt disjointed. My aim was to give a consistent experience across devices, reduce card height, & give a heirarchy to the information to enable improve scannability of content.
