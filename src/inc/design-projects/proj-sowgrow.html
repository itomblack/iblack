The Problem

How can I know when to grow and harvest my own vegetables

The Process

In 2015 I had two aims. Firstly, I wanted to improve my JavaScript knowledge. Secondly, I wanted to sample the delights of home grow vegetables in my dinner.

When I found the existing resources on growing vegetables to be lacklustre, I decided to create this app to help fulfil my needs. I gathered data from various resources, and got to work. As the codebase grew larger, so did my crops. You can watch my presentation on the whole experience below.
