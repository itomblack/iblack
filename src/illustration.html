Analogue may not be the future, but every once in a while I delight in reaching for good old fashioned pen, pencil and paper.

Why? Because, to quote another, drawing makes you see things clearer, and clearer and clearer still, until your eyes ache.

I have drawn for pleasure, for retail, for books and for weddings, and would love to do more!
